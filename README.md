### 👤 About Me
My journey into software engineering began with a simple curiosity about how the web works—a curiosity that has since evolved into over 3+ years of professional experience building complex digital products. As a frontend-focused engineer, I specialize in architecting ERP systems and developing scalable, high-performance web applications using React, Next.js & Vue 3.

I am passionate about the intersection of design and engineering. My favorite work involves turning intricate business flows into smooth, reliable user experiences. Whether I’m solving tricky UI behaviors, optimizing responsiveness, or integrating robust APIs, my goal is always to make complex systems feel simple and intuitive.

Beyond the frontend, I am a proficient Full-Stack & MERN Developer. This allows me to bridge the gap between client and server, building complete, end-to-end solutions using MongoDB, Express.js, and Node.js. Having a deep understanding of the full stack ensures that the applications I build are not only beautiful but also performant, secure, and architecturally sound from the ground up.
